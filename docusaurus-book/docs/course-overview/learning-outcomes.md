# Learning Outcomes

:::tip What You'll Master
By the end of this 13-week course on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics, you will have acquired comprehensive skills spanning fundamental concepts to advanced applications. These outcomes represent the essential competencies needed to work effectively in the field of embodied AI.
:::

## Core Concepts
- Understand Physical AI principles and embodied intelligence
- Explain the relationship between sensorimotor systems and intelligence
- Analyze the differences between traditional AI and embodied AI approaches

## Technical Skills
- Design and implement sensorimotor perception and manipulation systems
- Apply reinforcement learning algorithms for robot control
- Implement sim-to-real transfer techniques for robotics applications
- Develop humanoid robot kinematics and dynamics models
- Engineer bipedal locomotion and balance control systems
- Create manipulation and grasping solutions for humanoid hands
- Design natural human-robot interaction experiences

## Advanced Applications
- Integrate GPT models for conversational AI in robots
- Implement speech recognition and natural language understanding systems
- Create multi-modal interaction combining speech, gesture, and vision
- Develop ROS 2 packages for robotic applications
- Build Gazebo simulations for robot testing and validation
- Construct Isaac-based perception pipelines
- Deploy complete humanoid robots with conversational AI capabilities

## Practical Competencies
- Evaluate and select appropriate AI techniques for physical systems
- Troubleshoot and debug embodied AI systems
- Assess safety and ethical considerations in humanoid robotics
- Apply best practices for deploying AI in physical environments
- Integrate multiple subsystems into cohesive robotic solutions

:::note Assessment
These learning outcomes will be evaluated through a combination of practical projects, simulations, and the capstone project. Each outcome directly corresponds to specific assignments throughout the course.
:::
