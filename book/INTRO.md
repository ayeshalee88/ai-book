# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics: A Comprehensive Guide

![Book Cover Concept](https://placehold.co/800x400/4a86e8/ffffff?text=Physical+AI+%26+Humanoid+Robotics)

## About This Book

This comprehensive book bridges the gap between digital AI systems and physical robotic platforms. It covers the essential concepts, technologies, and practical implementations needed to develop embodied AI systems with a focus on humanoid robotics.

**Target Audience**: Intermediate to advanced readers including robotics engineers, AI/ML practitioners, researchers, students, and developers interested in embodied AI systems.

1. [Introduction to Embodied AI](./introduction/README.md) - Definitions, history, and why physical interaction matters
2. [Fundamentals of Physical AI](./fundamentals/README.md) - Sensorimotor coupling, perception-action loops, and bio-inspired designs
3. [Humanoid Robotics Design](./humanoid-robotics/README.md) - Design principles, kinematics, dynamics, and control systems
4. [AI Integration in Physical Systems](./ai-integration/README.md) - Using ML for locomotion, manipulation, and decision-making
5. [Case Studies](./case-studies/README.md) - Real-world applications like Boston Dynamics, Tesla Optimus, and open-source projects
6. [Challenges and Ethics](./challenges-ethics/README.md) - Safety, human-robot interaction, and societal impacts
7. [Tutorials and Hands-on Guides](./tutorials/README.md) - Practical implementation guides for simulation and hardware
8. [Deployment Best Practices](./deployment/README.md) - Testing, scaling, and real-world deployment strategies

- Python 3.7+ with packages: numpy, scipy, matplotlib, torch, opencv
- Robot Operating System (ROS/ROS2) for robotics examples
- Simulation environments: Gazebo, PyBullet
- Optional: Physical robot hardware for advanced tutorials
