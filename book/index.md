## Book Structure

This book is organized into progressive sections that build upon each other:

1. **Conceptual Foundation** - Understanding embodied AI and its importance
2. **Technical Fundamentals** - Core principles of physical AI systems
3. **Design and Implementation** - Practical approaches to humanoid robotics
4. **AI Integration** - How modern AI techniques apply to physical systems
5. **Real-World Applications** - Case studies and industry examples
6. **Ethical Considerations** - Safety, privacy, and societal impact
7. **Practical Implementation** - Tutorials and deployment strategies
