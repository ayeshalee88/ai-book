## Key Concepts Review

### 1. Embodied AI Fundamentals
- **Embodied Cognition**: Intelligence emerges from the dynamic interaction between an agent and its environment
- **Sensorimotor Coupling**: The tight relationship between sensing and motor activity
- **Perception-Action Loops**: Continuous cycles where sensory input drives motor output, which changes sensory input
- **Morphological Computation**: Using body structure and material properties to simplify control problems

### 2. Control Systems for Physical AI
- **Feedback Control**: Essential for stable physical interaction
- **PID Controllers**: Widely used for their simplicity and effectiveness
- **Model Predictive Control (MPC)**: Optimizes control actions over a finite horizon
- **Impedance Control**: Controls mechanical impedance for safe environment interaction

### 3. Humanoid Robotics Design
- **Kinematic Structure**: DOF configuration to replicate human mobility
- **Dynamic Modeling**: Understanding equations of motion for stable locomotion
- **Balance Control**: ZMP-based control, Capture Point control, and LIPM approaches
- **Whole-Body Control**: Coordinated motion of multiple task spaces

### 4. AI Integration
- **Reinforcement Learning**: Learning complex behaviors through trial and error
- **Imitation Learning**: Learning from demonstrations
- **Computer Vision**: Enabling robots to perceive and understand their environment
- **Large Language Models**: Natural human-robot interaction
